Bio

I was born and raised in San Diego, California. I received my BA in Neurobiology from UC Berkeley. While at Cal, I did brain imaging reserach in the Jagust Lab. I am now a Staff Research Associate in the Brewer Lab at the UC San Diego School of Medicine Department of Neurosciences. My primary focus is using neuroimaging to better understand neurological disorders. I am also a volunteer teacher for the League of Amazing Programmers, where I teach kids Java programming.
